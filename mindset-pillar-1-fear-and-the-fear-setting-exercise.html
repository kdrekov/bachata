Mindset Pillar 1: Fear (And the Fear Setting Exercise)

⬅️ Назад

Now we’re going to start covering the five pillars of mindset that, if practiced, will help you achieve more fulfillment in your dancing and in life.

Cultivating these pillars will make you a more effective human, saving you time, stress and enhancing your chances of success – at becoming the person (or dancer) you most want to be, and doing the things you most want to do.

 

Fear most often comes from fear of the unknown, so reduce the unknown and visualize everything that could go wrong.

Choose an action or decision you’re afraid to make and ask yourself “What is the worst thing that could happen? What would the consequences be?”

Column 1: Define – write down the worse case scenario and every bad thing you can think of that might happen

Column 2: Prevent – what could you do to prevent or reduce the chance of each thing happening?

Column 3: Repair – what could you do to repair the damage or who could you ask for help?

What might be the benefits or even a partial success? Consider them all. Take 10 minutes.

Make three columns and label them: 6 months, 12 months and 3 years.

Ask yourself: what will happen if I let fear win and avoid this action/decision? What might my life look like in 6 months, 12 months and 3 years?

Take it all in, and weigh the pros and cons of your action or decision.
