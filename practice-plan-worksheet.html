It’s time to take everything you’ve learned in this module and create a 1 page plan to reach your goals. I like to call this your success machine.

The objective of this machine is to set your trajectory and outline how you’re going to make progress toward your goals. The beauty of having a process, a machine, is that you can adjust it anytime. If you don’t like the outputs you’re getting, just change the inputs. And trust me, you will need to make adjustments as you go.
