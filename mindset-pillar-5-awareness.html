Mindset Pillar 5: Self Awareness (3 Levels)

⬅️ Назад

These are three levels of awareness you want to reflect on and cultivate:

This is your core beliefs and values: WHY you do what you do and WHY you want the things you want.

This is your habits and behaviours: HOW you approach something.

This is your goals and desires: WHAT you want.

Reflect on each of these for yourself to become more aware. You can’t change or improve something until you’re aware of it.

You can’t self-correct and adjust course until you know where the problem lies.
