Pass By (Footwork)

⬅️ Назад

In this lesson we’re doing solo footwork. Footwork is great to practice timing, steps, coordination and musicality without worrying about leading and following. In the next lesson we’ll show you how you can translate these steps into partner work.

Jhaime is a great bachata dancer from Montreal, Canada and he’s going to be guest instructing alongside Regan in various lessons in the program.
