Small Habits, Big Results & the Problem With Goals

If you can control your behaviour, you can do anything.

The challenge for most of us is the motivations of our behaviour lurk under the surface and we barely understand why we do the things we do.

If you take the time to become aware of your habits and learn how to design your behaviour you’ll amaze yourself at what you can change and achieve, in life and dancing.
